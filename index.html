About Us

AMM PACKAGING a marketing company established in 2008

providing turn key solution to their valuable customers with all types of packaging. Having an alliance with one of the most renowned companies – PURITY FLEXPACK LTD. , SHUBHAM PACKAGING , JIGAR PALLET SYSTEM AND MAKERS POLY SHRINK, it serves various needs of FMCG, CHEMICAL, PHARMA, PETROCHEMICAL, LOGISTICS AND WAREHOUSING industries. In Indian market AMM Packaging has built its own repo by providing competitive prices, reliable & trustworthy services fulfilling all the needs of customers.

We believe in customer satisfaction

AMM Packaging is the most trustworthy name in providing the best packaging material. Our products are tailored under advanced machineries and equipment, fulfilling the market gap and serving clients under given delivery date.

Products purchased from AMM Packaging are known for their exceptional surface finish, high strength, optimum durability and reliability. Hence, AMM Packaging is prime in providing turn-key solution for packaging in terms of service, quality and price in the market.
